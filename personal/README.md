# Personal Operations

Personal life, goals, health, and non-business tasks.

## Structure

```
personal/
  CLAUDE.md              Personal workspace overview
  instruction.md         Setup guide (5-phase, 90 min)
  plan.md                Your values, goals, what matters
  personal-ops.md        Active tasks, recurring habits
  inbox.md               Quick captures (emptied daily)
  health/
    tracking.md          Health metrics & energy patterns
```

## Key Files

**plan.md** – Your operating principles
- This year's focus (1 thing)
- Values (3-5 things that guide decisions)
- Health goals
- Personal goals (non-business)
- What success looks like

**personal-ops.md** – Your working memory
- Active tasks (what you're working on)
- Recurring habits (exercise, family time, routines)
- Health tracking
- Avoiding items (things you know you should do but are procrastinating on)

**inbox.md** – Capture-without-judgment
- Ideas that pop up
- Things you want to do but not now
- Personal reminders
- Cleared daily by standup

**health/tracking.md** – What affects your energy
- Medications/supplements
- Exercise and goals
- Sleep patterns
- Energy patterns (when you have it, when you lose it)
- Seasonal variations
- Allergies and triggers

## How It Works

**Daily (Standup):**
- Reads plan.md (alignment check: "Are we doing work that matches your values?")
- Reads personal-ops.md (what personal work to suggest today?)
- Reads health/tracking.md (what's your energy state? What conditions affect you?)
- Processes inbox.md (captures routed to appropriate places)

**Weekly (Retrospective):**
- Reviews energy patterns
- Asks: "What affected your energy this week?"
- Updates memory with patterns

**Quarterly:**
- Revisit plan.md
- Are these goals still true?
- Should you adjust focus or values?

## Integration

Personal tasks are weighted alongside business tasks. The system doesn't treat them as "extra" or "if you have time."

Examples:
- Low energy day? Suggest personal admin instead of deep work
- High pollen day? Suggest indoor personal tasks
- Running is a personal goal? Suggest it on nice-weather days

## Quick Start

1. **Read CLAUDE.md** – Understand how personal ops integrate
2. **Run instruction.md** – Follow the 5-phase setup (90 minutes total)
3. **Update weekly** – Add to status-log in personal-ops.md
4. **Review quarterly** – Check if plan.md still reflects your values

## Philosophy

Personal operations are as important as business operations. If you're burned out and unhappy, the business doesn't work either.

This workspace ensures that:
- Personal wellbeing is integrated into daily planning (not an afterthought)
- You're working toward goals that actually matter to you
- Your health and energy are understood and respected
- Your values guide your decisions

---

**Your personal ops are the foundation. Everything else builds on this.**

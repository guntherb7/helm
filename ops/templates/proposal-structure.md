---

**Notes:**
- Keep this professional but warm (like talking to a smart friend)
- Be specific about what they get (not vague "development work")
- Anticipate questions (that's what the Assumptions section is for)
- Make next steps crystal clear (they should know exactly what to do)
- Include your contact info (email + phone if you want)

**Word Count:** Aim for 2-4 pages. Longer proposals feel overwhelming. Shorter feels like you didn't think it through.

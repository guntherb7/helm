## Factors That Increase Price
- Custom block/component development
- E-commerce or payment integration
- Complex content modeling
- Migration from existing site
- Training and documentation
- Ongoing maintenance agreement

## Factors That Decrease Price
- Using existing theme with minimal customization
- Simple content structure
- Client provides all content ready to go
- Smaller scope / fewer pages

## Industry Terms

### Web Development
- **CMS:** Content Management System (WordPress, Drupal, etc.)
- **API:** Application Programming Interface (system-to-system communication)
- **Custom post types:** WordPress data structure for non-standard content
- **Plugin:** WordPress/CMS extension that adds functionality
- **Theme:** Visual template for WordPress/CMS
- **Staging environment:** Copy of live site used for testing before deployment
- **CDN:** Content Delivery Network (speeds up static asset delivery)
- **Database normalization:** Organizing data to reduce redundancy
- **Migration:** Moving a site from one platform to another

### Business Terms
- **Net 30:** Payment due 30 days from invoice date
- **Milestone:** Completion point with payment trigger
- **Scope creep:** Adding work beyond original agreement
- **Change order:** Formal request to modify scope (usually triggers cost increase)
- **Go-live:** Production launch date
- **UAT:** User Acceptance Testing (client testing before live launch)
- **Retainer:** Ongoing monthly fee for availability/maintenance
- **Hourly rate:** Charged per hour of work
- **Fixed price:** Quote for entire project regardless of hours

### Legal Terms
- **IP assignment:** Transfer of ownership of intellectual property to client
- **Warranty:** Guarantee about the work (typically 30 days)
- **Limitation of liability:** Cap on damages in case of issues
- **Indemnification:** Agreement to cover legal costs/damages
- **Non-compete:** Restriction on working with competitors
- **Confidentiality:** Agreement not to share client information

### Acme Corp

## Voice Consistency Checklist

When writing proposals, emails, and contracts, remember:

- [ ] Do you call it "WordPress" or "WP" or "custom CMS"?
- [ ] Do you say "deliverables" or "outputs" or "milestones"?
- [ ] Do you use "engagement" or "project" or "contract"?
- [ ] Do you write "you'll get" or "deliverable includes" or "we provide"?
- [ ] Do you say "work with you" or "partner with" or "build for you"?

Consistency builds trust and brand recognition.

## Annual Review

Each January, review this year's position:

- Total revenue for the year
- Total expenses (monthly + annual)
- Tax paid or owed
- Cash remaining
- Average monthly revenue
- Rate of growth
- Payment patterns from clients
- Decisions for next year (price increase? New services? Target revenue?)

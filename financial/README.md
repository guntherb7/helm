# Financial Operations

Business financial tracking, invoicing, and cash flow management.

## Structure

```
financial/
  CLAUDE.md              Financial workspace overview
  instruction.md         Setup guide
  financial-ops.md       Invoice tracking & expense calendar
  accounts.md            Account summary & cash position
  pricing.md             Current rates & pricing history
  tax-prep.md            Tax documents & checklist
```

## Key Files

**financial-ops.md** – Your working memory
- Invoice status (sent, paid, outstanding)
- Monthly cash flow
- Recurring expenses
- Payment follow-ups needed

**accounts.md** – Monthly snapshot
- Accounts receivable (money owed to you)
- Accounts payable (money you owe)
- Cash position
- Tax reserve

**pricing.md** – Rate reference
- Current project rates
- Hourly rates (if applicable)
- Historical pricing
- Rate increase decisions

**tax-prep.md** – Tax checklist
- Timeline for gathering documents
- Deduction checklist
- Quarterly estimated tax calculation
- Accountant contact

## How It Works

**Daily:**
- No daily financial work (handled monthly)

**Weekly (Friday):**
- Check `financial-ops.md` for invoice status
- Flag any overdue invoices (older than 35 days)
- Verify recurring expenses cleared

**Monthly (Last Friday):**
- Update invoice status
- Add any new invoices sent
- Record payment dates
- Update cash position in `accounts.md`
- Verify recurring expenses

**Quarterly:**
- Review accounts.md
- Check revenue vs goals
- Review pricing (rate increase needed?)
- Calculate quarterly estimated tax

**Annually (January):**
- Gather tax documents
- Work with accountant for tax return
- Review pricing history
- Plan rate increases for next year

## Integration

**Retrospective (Weekly):**
- Reads `financial-ops.md`
- Asks: "Cash flow status? Any payment issues?"
- Updates patterns in memory

**Standup (Daily):**
- Checks for critical cash flow issues
- Affects work prioritization if payment delayed

**Follow-up (Automatic):**
- Detects overdue invoices
- Drafts payment reminder emails

## Philosophy

Financial clarity reduces stress. You don't need fancy software—just honest tracking.

When you know:
- What's owed to you (and when it's due)
- What you owe (and when it's due)
- What's actually in the bank

...you can make better decisions.

## Quick Start

1. **Read CLAUDE.md** – Understand the system
2. **Run instruction.md** – Follow the setup phases
3. **Update monthly** – Last Friday of each month, update `financial-ops.md`
4. **Check quarterly** – Review `accounts.md` and pricing
5. **Integrate with retrospective** – Answer the finance questions during weekly review

## Template Files

All files in this directory are templates. Customize with:
- Your actual rates and business model
- Your recurring expenses
- Your tax situation
- Your accounting approach

No personally identifying information should be in these files (use generic client names, round numbers if privacy is a concern).
